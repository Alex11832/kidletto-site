# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 25, 2025**

## Introduction

Kidletto ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:
- Child's name, age, gender, and eye color
- Your email address
- Photos you upload for story personalization
- Information about your child's interests and learning goals
- Payment information (processed through secure third-party providers)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Browser type and version
- IP address
- Pages visited and time spent
- Device information
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Create personalized children's stories
- Process and fulfill orders
- Send order confirmations and story download links
- Improve our services and website
- Comply with legal obligations
- Send marketing communications (if you opt-in)

## 3. Photo Usage and Storage

### Photo Protection
- Your child's photos are used solely for creating personalized story illustrations
- Photos are processed using secure, encrypted connections
- We do not share photos with third parties without explicit consent
- Photos are stored securely and deleted after story generation (unless you request otherwise)

### Illustration Integration
- Your child's likeness is integrated into custom illustrations using AI technology
- These illustrations are unique to your order and not used for any other purpose
- You retain ownership of the stories created with your photos

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant providers
- Regular security audits and updates
- Limited employee access to personal information

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:
- Payment processing
- Email delivery
- Cloud storage
- Analytics

These providers are contractually obligated to maintain data confidentiality and security.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Kidletto is designed for parents and guardians to create stories about their children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by:
- Requiring parental consent for all data collection
- Not collecting information directly from children
- Providing parents control over their child's information

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kidletto.com

## 8. Retention Policy

- Personal information is retained for as long as necessary to provide services
- Photos are deleted 90 days after order completion (unless you request archival)
- You may request deletion at any time

## 9. International Users

If you access Kidletto from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

## 10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or prominent notice on our website.
